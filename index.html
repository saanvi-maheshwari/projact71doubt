  • Shiv Nadar School GOOD Review

    I’m a huge fan of the Shiv Nadar Foundation, they’ve got 2 top schools one in Noida and one in Gurgaon. As you can imagine with the resources at their disposal (Shiv Nadar was founder of HCL) the schools are going to be top of the line. Part of the reason I believe that is that Mr. Nadar is big on philanthropy, he’s committed $1bn to his foundation which is focused solely on education. So he’s clearly not in it to turn a profit and therefore won’t cut corners when it comes to delivering quality education. In my opinion, it’s the best school to bet on because it’s the one place that will put every penny you pay into your child’s education since they don’t care for profit. They’ve only completed their first year but the response from parents has been very good.You should check out their website: shivnadarschool.edu.in
